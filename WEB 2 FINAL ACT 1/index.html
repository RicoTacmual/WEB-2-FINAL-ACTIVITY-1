Contact Storage App

Introducing Contact Storage App - organize and manage your contacts hassle-free.

Add Contacts

With Contact Storage App, you can easily add new contacts to your collection. Click the "Add Contact" link in the navigation menu to get started. Simply fill out the form with the contact's information and click "Save".

Edit Contacts

Need to update contact information? Use the "Edit Contact" link in the navigation menu to modify an existing contact. Find the contact you want to edit and click "Edit" to make your changes.

Delete Contacts

If you no longer need a contact in your collection, simply delete it. Use the "Delete Contact" link in the navigation menu to remove a contact. Find the contact you want to delete and click "Delete".
